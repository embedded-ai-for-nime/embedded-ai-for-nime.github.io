Embedded AI for NIME: Challenges and Opportunities

Workshop Proposal for NIME 2022

Dates: Subject to acceptance.
Organisers: Teresa Pelinski, Victor Shepardson, Steve Symons, Franco Santiago Caspe, Adan L Benito Temprano, Jack Armitage, Chris Kiefer, Rebecca Fiebrink, Thor Magnusson, and Andrew McPherson

Welcome

With this workshop we aim to bring together researchers with the common interest of working with and developing embedded AI for NIMEs, and to jointly identify the challenges, trends, and technologies in use.

SHORT OVERVIEW OF THE PROGRAMME : TALKS / PRESENTATIONS / DISCUSSIONS

The workshop will run for half a day and will be repeated for different time-zones.
We aim to group the selected presenters under similar topics, however these topics are not proposed in advance in hope that those will emerge by themselves in the submissions.

Call for contributions

We will send an open call for abstracts / short papers / progress reports / demos / posters / short talks with themes that could include (but not necessarily restricted to):

  • Any technical implementation of embedded systems using AI in the context of NIME ?
  • Design strategies for embedded AI ?
  • Interaction paradigms or systems using embedded AI ?
  • Embedded neural audio synthesis ?
  • AR/VR systems using AI in the context of NIME ?
  • Musical uses of AI in embedded platforms ?
  • Workflows&software for moving AI implementations between laptop, embedded/realtime and HPC platforms.
  • Values, biases and ethical issues with embedded AI for musical performance
